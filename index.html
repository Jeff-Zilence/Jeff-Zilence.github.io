Sijie Zhu is now a PhD student of UCF with Prof. Chen Chen. He received his master's degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, and bachelor's degree from University of Science and Technology of China. During summer 2021, he was a research intern in Adobe Research, working with Zhe Lin, Scott Cohen, Zhifei Zhang, Jason Kuen.
